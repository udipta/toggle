About Us

We are a GLUG composed of students from the department of Computer Science and Engineering, who share a common vision and philosophy of the Free Software Foundation.

How we got started



We are a GLUG under FSMK. A few Seniors from our depertment, Dept. of Computer Science initiated the GLUG in 2011 inspired by the philosophy of FOSS

Our goal is to promote and encourage the use of Free Software and Hardware & bring in more upstream contributors to various FOSS projects. Through our glug we show the path of becoming an upstream contributor.
