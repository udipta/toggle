About Us

We are a GLUG composed of students from the department Computer Science and Engineering, who share a common vision and philosophy of the Free Software Foundation.

How we got started



Actually, we are a part of FSMK. A few Seniors from our depertment, Dept. of Computer Science started in 2011 as they liked the philosophy of FOSS

Our goal is to promote and encourage the use of Free Software and Hardware & bring in more upstream contributors to various FOSS projects. Through our glug we show the path of becoming an upstream contributor.
